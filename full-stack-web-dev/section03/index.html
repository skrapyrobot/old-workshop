Angela's Recipe for the Best Cinnamon Rolls

This is the best one you are going to find in the wild internet

Ingredients

In order to make this recipe come true, you have to get the following:

For the dough:

  • ⅓ cup warm milk.
  • 2¼ teaspoons yeast.
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar.
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk.
  • ¼ cup butter.
  • 3 cups bread flour.

That's literally all you need for this one!

For the filling:

  • ⅔ cup dark brown sugar
  • 1½ tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • ¼ cup butter

Don't forget the cinnamon!!!
